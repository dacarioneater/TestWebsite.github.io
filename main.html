Q&A

How can I print my transcript report?

After entering the desired grades, click on the “Generate GPA Grade Report” icon; a new tab containing your transcript in a form of pdf will pop up. Click the print icon and now you have all your grades on paper!

What is the difference between a 100 Point Scale and 4.0 Point Scale?

100 and 4.0 Point Scales are the most frequently used GPA scales. Some institutes prefer to use one over the other. However, upon applications to different schools, you may be required to submit your GPA using a specific scale.

Why do I receive two different types of GPA? Which one is my real GPA, unweighted or weighted?

Unweighted and weighted GPAs are both considered your real GPA.Their difference is in their way of measuring students’ academic performance; weighted GPA weighs the difficulty of your classes; in other words, it takes into account the rigor of courses and awards bonus points upon completion and passing higher level courses such as AP/IB/Honors. On the other hand, an unweighted GPA does not consider the rigor of courses but rather measures the raw scores you achieved (it does not award any bonus points!).

What does the semester icon refer to?

The “semester” icon indicates the semester that you have completed the course, (ex: first semester).
